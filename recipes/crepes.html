These cream-filled berry boosters of joy are a joy to make and even more of a joy to shove down your pie hole!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Whisk together milk, eggs, and salt.
  2. Gradually whisk in the flour and butter.
  3. Scoop batter into a hot griddle.
  4. Cook until lightly browned on the bottom.
  5. Flip and continue cooking until done on both sides.

Filling ideas!

You can fill your crepes with whatever delicious fillings you'd like! Whipped cream, cream cheese (ew), Nutella, fruit sauces, you name it!
