Delicious Homemade Lamb Gyros!

A delicious and easy to prepare lamb gyro!

These scrummy mummy wummy lamb gyros are bound to tear the roof off of and house and/or function you bring this meal to! There will be nothing left but the nostalgia of the first bite and the woes of broken spirits with no home without the gyro!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 medium onion, chopped into chunks
  • 1Lb ground lamb
  • 1lb 80/20 ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried marjoram
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon ground dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons fine sea salt

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Pulse onion in food processor until finely chopped. Scoop onions into the center of a towel, gather the towel ends and squeeze to remove liquids.
  3. Combine onion, lamb, beef, garlic, oregano, cumin, marjoram, rosemary, thyme, pepper, and salt into a large bowl. Mix with hands until well combined. Cover and refridgerate for 1-2 hours.
  4. Preheat oven to 325 degreed F (165 degrees C). Evenly coat a 9x5-inch loaf pan with cooking spray.
  5. Working in 2 batches if needed, place meat mixture into a food processor and pulse until finely chopped.
  6. Transfer mixture into prepared loaf pan and pack down to make sure there are no air pockets. Line a medium roasting pan with a damp kitchen towel. Place loaf into the center of the roasting pan and transfer into preheated oven. Carefully pour boiling water into the roastig pan until it comes halfway up the sides of the loaf pan.
  7. Bake until gyro meat is no longer pink in the center (about 1 hour - 1 hour 25 minutes). A thermometer inserted into the center should read atleast 160 degrees F (70 degrees C)
  8. Pour off any accumulated fat and allow to cool before slicing and serving.
  9. Enjoy!
