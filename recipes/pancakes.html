Pancakes

A beautiful stack of pancakes ready to be made at home!

These homemade pancakes are bound to make anyone who eats them flourish with power and thank the heavens for descending a cooking angel down on them. These fluffy delicious down-right delights are easy to make and easier to eat!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Sift dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well and add wet ingredients, stir to combine.
  3. Scoop batter onto hot griddle or pan
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes before flipping.
  5. Continue cooking until both sides are brown
    • Tip: When your pancake is ready to be flipped, bubbles will start to form at the top!
